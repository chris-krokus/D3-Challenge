Smokers vs. Income By State

Correlations Discovered Between Annual Income and Percentage of People Who Smoke

The state with the highest percentage of the population that smokes is West Virginia, at 26.7%, and the average income of someone who lives there is just over $41k.

The state with the lowest percentage of the population that smokes is Utah, at 9.7%, and the average income of someone who lives there is just under $61k.

If West Virginia residents were to quit smoking, that would save them hundreds of dollars per year, bringing them closer to the quality of life experienced by Utah residents.
